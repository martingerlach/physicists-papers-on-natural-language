About this page

We aim at listing all papers by physicists, or published in physics journals, on natural language from a complex systems viewpoint. This web page is maintained by Eduardo G. Altmann and Martin Gerlach. Please help us sending papers that belong to this list to martin.gerlach (AT) northwestern.edu, or by contributing to the corresponding group "Physics papers in natural languages" in Mendeley.

We have collected 452 papers from 1989 to 2016   |   Last modified on 03 Jan 2017.
